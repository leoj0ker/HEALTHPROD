Stand tall with your hands by your sides, feet shoulder-width apart, and toes pointed forward. Keeping your back flat and core braced, push your hips back, bend your knees, and lower your body until your thighs are parallel to the floor. You want to “sit” into the exercise, pushing your butt back like you’re lowering yourself onto a chair or bench. Never bend forward at your waist — that will only increase the stress on your spine and throw you off balance. Pause, and then push yourself back up to the starting position.
