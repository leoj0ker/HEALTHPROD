To do a Burpee you stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and arms by your sides. Squat and reach forward to place your hands on the floor, shoulder-width apart. Kick your legs straight out behind you into a high plank with your hands stacked underneath your shoulders. Bend your elbows to lower your chest to the floor, then straighten them to do a push-up. Jump your feet toward your hands so your lower body is in a squat. Jump straight up into the air, reaching your arms overhead. That's 1 rep.
