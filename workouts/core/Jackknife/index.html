Lengthen your body on your mat. Lie flat on your back and neutralize your low back (avoid a rounded or an arched spine). Extend your legs out long and reach your arms above your head. Hover your arms and legs a few inches off the mat (still press your tailbone into the mat). Take a deep breath, and on your exhale lift your head and pull your arms and legs up into a V-shape (lift roughly 30-45 degrees away from the mat). On your lift, keep your legs straight and your arms parallel to your thighs. Lower back down to your starting position. Repeat these steps for 10-12 reps and 2-3 sets.
